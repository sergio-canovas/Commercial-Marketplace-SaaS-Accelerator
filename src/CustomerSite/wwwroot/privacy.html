Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Data Sovereignty and Processing

The dForts Copilot Readiness Monitor is designed with a "Privacy by Design" architecture. All scanning of SharePoint site permissions, files, and metadata is performed locally within your browser using Microsoft Graph and SharePoint APIs. No SharePoint content, file metadata, or permission records are ever transmitted to or stored on dForts' servers.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect the minimum information necessary to manage your subscription and licensing:

  • Tenant ID: To identify your Microsoft 365 organization and validate your subscription status.
  • User Email: Collected only during the account configuration phase for administrative contact and support purposes.
  • Subscription Status: Information regarding your active plan (Trial, Standard, or Premium).

3. Data Storage

Scan results, risk scores, and remediation logs are stored exclusively within your own SharePoint Online environment in hidden lists created by the application. dForts has no access to this data. For trial tracking purposes, only the Tenant ID and Trial Start Date are stored in our secure database.

4. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect the licensing information stored in our Azure environment. We do not sell or share your information with third parties.
